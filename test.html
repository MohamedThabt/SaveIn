How to properly reload:

Step 1: Open a new tab and go to chrome://extensions/
Step 2: Find "LinkedIn Saver" in the list
Step 3: Toggle the extension OFF (blue switch → grey)
Step 4: Wait 2 seconds
Step 5: Toggle it back ON (grey switch → blue)
Step 6: Go to LinkedIn and press Ctrl + Shift + R
Step 7: Open console (F12) → look for "LinkedIn Saver Diagnostics" messages

If toggling off/on doesn't work, click "Remove" on the extension, then click "Load unpacked" again and select the e:\linked in extention folder.
